PlayStation

What is PlayStation

PlayStation games have become very popular in recent times, and have become one of the most popular ways of entertainment among children and young people alike; Where PlayStation is known as a brand of a series of video game systems, and appeared for the first time in 1994; It was developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment, a subsidiary of the leading electronics company, Sony, and has since been able to upend the concepts related to the game industry and development, through four consecutive and innovative generations, and is now the number one home entertainment platform in the world, responsible for a proportion of 78% of Sony's profits.
